Launching the scenario

To get everything up we just have to run:

python3 cloudgoat.py create ec2_ssrf

The next thing we should do is validate that the credentials work. The get caller identity is a great command for this. This call is the equivilant to 'whaomi' in the aws hypervisor.

aws sts get-caller-identity

A response should come back with letting us know that we are the solus user.
